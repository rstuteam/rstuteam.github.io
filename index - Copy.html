6 of Us

Animation
Department

The first ever department from this studio. As the name suggests, this department's responsibility is to make an Animation that follows the studio's standard and makes "therealrstu's Imagination" become real through 2D and 3D animation.

Film
Department

Established in 2022, this department's focus is on producing real-life films. With help from Big Bryan Production, this department successfully made an award-winning film and received 2 awards from a film competition.

Audio
Department

Established in 2024, it focuses on making audio, songs, and everything related to audio and sound. This department isn't yet ready for the production because of their "Young Age" and still need more people that capable with sound and music.

Cyber-Security
Department

Established in 2022, focuses on making websites and securing them. This department also controls this website and several other websites for security reasons. One of them is from a Little Intelligent Agency from a middle school.

Software
Department

Established in 2021, this department already makes a lot of applications, one of them is a monitor recorder. But since the hacker attack in 2023, this department has been shutting down for several moments in the future.

Connection
Department

Established in 2024, this department has to search and make a relation with other teams or companies for the studio's needs. This department will be helpful when there is a big project from the Animation or Film department.
